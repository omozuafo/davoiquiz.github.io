- Our Vision -

We envision a future where every Nigerian secondary school student is equipped with essential ICT knowledge and skills, empowering them to thrive in the digital age. By hosting the national ICT quiz, we aspire to inspire a new generation of tech-savvy individuals who will drive innovation, contribute to the growth of Nigeria's technology sector, and make a positive impact on society.

About Us

Founded in 2002, the NGO is a non-profit organization with the aim of advancing the technical expertise and values associated with the use of information and communication technologies, bringing awareness to pandemics, and combating global climate change at grassroots levels. Founded in 2007, the organization has been the brainchild of a group of Electrical Engineers, Computer Scientists, Production Engineers, Epidemiologists, others professionals, and prominent visionaries who are committed to the ideals of advancing technology in ICT, preventing disease outbreaks, and encouraging a friendly environment that promotes the well-being of all people.

Our Aim

Our aim is to identify, manage and design high quality Information Communication Technology (ICT) syllabus and curriculum for schools, colleges and other professional institutions in Nigeria. Moreover, we are also aiming at implementing ICT strategies for schools and to prepare and deliver training, allowing them to fully utilize information, communication and technology to its fullest potential in the educational environment in Nigeria.

- MANAGEMENT -

The organization is made up Directors who are professionals in their various fields of endeavor; driven by the passion to meet a need in the area of ICT technological advancement, prevention of disease outbreaks, encourage friendly environment and well being for all humanity. Our multidisciplinary and highly skilled Management team works closely with other partners to identify key strategic and management issues and challenges. We deliver effective and responsive performance management system and monitoring capabilities through innovative application of our balance scorecard
